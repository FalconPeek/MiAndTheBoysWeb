# Project: PickEmMundialLosguRise

## Vision
An exclusive, professional web platform for the "MiAndTheBoys" group of friends. It gamifies social interaction through FIFA-style card voting, World Cup 2026 sports predictions, and an internal "Guribet" economy.

## Core Mandates
- **Exclusivity:** Strictly limited to 11 predefined users.
- **Performance:** Low-latency, minimal resources. No heavy cryptography.
- **Aesthetic:** High-quality, dynamic animations (CSS 3D/Three.js) with a hybrid FIFA/Group identity.
- **Economy:** Centralized virtual currency (GuriCoins) connecting all modules.
